# Hey, I'm Jeff 👋

I'm a Technical Recruiter based in New York.

Over the last several years I've recruited across software engineering, infrastructure, AI/ML, data, security, and product teams at both startups and larger technology organizations.

Most of my career has been spent helping engineering teams hire exceptional talent.

Along the way, I became increasingly interested in the questions behind the hiring process:

* Where do great engineers come from?
* What experiences shape technical talent?
* Why do certain organizations consistently produce strong engineers?
* What signals actually matter when evaluating talent?

This GitHub is where I organize that research and continue learning.

## A Few Things I've Learned

* Strong engineers are often continuous learners.
* Open-source participation can be a powerful signal, but context matters.
* Exceptional talent rarely develops in isolation.
* Great engineering organizations create environments where people can grow.
* The best recruiters understand both people and systems.
